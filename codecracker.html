How to Play

Find four groups of four related media terms from the Year 12 MPA syllabus.

Tap four terms you think belong to the same category, then press Submit. If you're correct, the group is revealed. If not, you lose a life.

You have 4 mistakes allowed. If three of your four selections are correct, you'll get an "One away…" hint.

Groups are colour-coded by difficulty: Blue is the most straightforward, then Green, Gold, and Red is the trickiest.

Use Shuffle to rearrange the tiles — sometimes a fresh layout helps you spot connections. Use Deselect to clear your current selection.

Built for Sacred Heart College Year 12 ATAR MPA revision. Terms drawn from Units 3 & 4 syllabus content.
